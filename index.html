About Us

At Lotus Recovery, our mission is to foster a transformative environment nurturing personal growth, fostering mentorship, and cultivating essential life skills while prioritizing family support. Grounded in our commitment to empower individuals towards a purposeful life and unwavering sobriety, we deeply value and acknowledge the healing journeys undertaken by each individual on their path to recovery.

Our Program

Residents at Lotus Recovery can expect a supportive community focused on independent living and personal development. Our living arrangements are designed to provide a serene and stable environment, while our daily schedules and support services ensure that each resident has the tools they need for success. We prioritize family support, including facilitating reunification for women seeking to regain custody after achieving sobriety, and we welcome pets as part of the healing process.
